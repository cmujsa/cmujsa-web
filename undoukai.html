Undoukai (NEW!!)

For the first time since 2014, JSA hosted an Undoukai (運動会) on November 4th, 2023! Undoukai is a traditional sports day festival held annually by schools in Japan. As part of our Undoukai, Red Team and White Team competed in a fun mix of traditional events and club favorite games.
